# Report Design Preferences

All job codes have separate report files.

Use this guide to define how each report should look, feel, and function. Rate each item from **0** to **5**, where **0** is the simplest or lowest-priority option and **5** is the most polished, detailed, or highest-priority option.

## Visual Style & Density

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Visual polish | Very plain and basic | Highly polished and modern |  5|
| Layout density | Spacious and easy to read | Dense and information-heavy |  5|
| Color usage | Mostly black, white, and gray | Strong use of colors for sections or status |  5|

## Information & Structure

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Information visibility | Minimal summary only | Everything visible without clicking |  5|
| Section separation | Simple continuous layout | Strongly divided sections or cards | 5|
| Key metric emphasis | All information has equal weight | Totals, costs, deadlines, and key numbers stand out | 5 |

## Navigation & Interaction

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Interactivity | Static, like a printed report | Interactive with toggles, dropdowns, or filters | 3 |
| Quick navigation | Basic scrolling only | Jump links, sidebar navigation, or sticky headers | 3 |

## Readability & Usability

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Text size | Smaller text to fit more information | Larger text for easier reading |  5|
| At-a-glance readability | Takes time to read carefully | Instantly scannable | 5 |

## Media & Detail

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Image usage | No images | Heavy use of job site photos or diagrams |  5|
| Section detail | Short summaries | Detailed breakdowns |  0|

## Practical Use

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Print friendliness | Viewed only on screen | Prints cleanly like a PDF | 5 |
| Mobile importance | Desktop only | Mobile viewing is very important |  5|

## Priority & Feel

| Preference | 0 Means | 5 Means | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Speed vs. presentation | Fastest to load and use | Best-looking, even if slightly heavier |  5|
